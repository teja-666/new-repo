The Monstera deliciosa, also known as the Swiss Cheese Plant, is a popular tropical houseplant known for its unique, large, perforated leaves.

Care Tips

  • Light: Bright, indirect light is best. Direct sun can burn the leaves.
  • Water: Water thoroughly when the top two inches of soil feel dry.
  • Soil: Use a well-draining potting mix.
  • Humidity: Prefers high humidity, but it's not strictly necessary.
